Shop Story Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 21, 2024

Introduction

Shop Story ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Shop Mini.

Information We Collect

Shop Story accesses the following information through the Shopify Shop platform:

  • Saved Products: We access your saved/favorited products to analyze your style preferences and shopping habits.
  • Recommended Products: We access Shopify's product recommendations to provide personalized insights about your future style evolution.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Analyze your shopping aesthetics and style preferences
  • Generate personalized insights about your shopping patterns
  • Provide recommendations and style analysis
  • Estimate environmental impact of your product selections

AI Processing

Product information is processed using Google's Gemini AI to generate style insights and recommendations. Product data is sent to Google's API for analysis purposes only and is not stored permanently by third-party services.

Data Storage

We do not permanently store your personal information or product data on our servers. All analysis is performed in real-time, and results are displayed directly to you within the Shop Story mini.

Third-Party Services

Shop Story uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini AI: For generating style insights and product analysis
  • Supabase: For secure API key management (optional)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data within the Shop platform
  • Stop using Shop Story at any time
  • Request information about how your data is used

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Shopify Shop platform.
